The Lexus IS200’s service manual

  • en
  • is200
  • service

Mr. Bidouille - 2020-05-01

The service manual is an essential tool to service your car. But the problem is that it can be hard to find. Especially for a 20 years old car.

But I finally got my hands on it!

I had to recreate the PDF files, because the pages were scattered in a number of files, mixed in with a few pages about the IS300 and the Sportcross version of the car. It wasn't an easy task, but the hours spent in the train were at least used for good.

So if you want my complete and recreated version of the IS200's service manual, I invite you to send me an email at hello (at) mrbidouille.ch . I'll answer with a download link as soon as possible (~260MB compressed, ~300MB decompressed, 2'078 elements).


I do not simply upload it to the blog because I don't think that would be very legal.


This was a very short article, but fear not. Many interesting things are coming ! Including a rather complete guide (I hope) on how to do the timing belt on the IS200.
