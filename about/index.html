In English first (français en dessous)

Hey. thanks for checking out my blog !
I'm from Switzerland and speak both French and English, that is why this site's interface is in English and most articles are available in both languages.

This blog is a way for me to record things that I do for fun, and share my "experience" (I'm such a noob, don't expect much when it comes to cars) (I'm quite good at IT though, hehe).
By the way, the verb "bidouiller" (from which the noun "bidouille" comes) is mainly translated to English as "to hack".
But it really means more than that in French. To me at least, it means to try to understand topics/machines you don't know by just giving it a go. It means to get your hands dirty and get things done, even if it isn't professionally done.

I'll stay mostly anonymous. If you want to know the information i cared to share about myself, check out my introduction post here ( en / fr).

If you want to get in touch for whatever reason, don't hesitate to drop a mail at hello (at) mrbidouille.ch .

Stickers !

I have stickers, that you can obtain by meeting with me, or when buying the various things I will be selling in the short future.
I would be touched if you decided on using them :)

Some formalities

For my own protection, I have to say that whatever information you find on this site is given without guaranty. I am not a professional, and as such, nothing I say/write should be taken as truth or advice.
Always stay safe when working on cars and with electronics, and when in doubt, ask a professional.
I do not condone reckless driving on public roads. Always follow traffic rules and try not to do something stupid. As a community, our image depends on it.

To have a better idea of how many people visit my blog, I records the fact that you visited it at a certain time, and I capture your user agent, which contains information your browser shares with the web, like your web browser version, language and device.
I do not keep any other data about you, and I use that data anonymously, solely for statistical reasons.
I respect the "Do Not Track" flag that you can enable in your browser. If the flag is enabled, from my point of view, it is as if you never visited the website.

Quelques formalités

Pour ma propre protection, je dois mentionner que les informations que vous trouverez sur ce site sont données sans garantie. Je ne suis pas un professionnel et, de ce fait, aucune information que je dis/écris ne doit être considérée comme vraie ou comme un conseil

Je respect l'option "Ne pas me pister" que vous pouvez activer dans votre navigateur. Si l'option est activée, de mon point de vue, c'est comme si vous n'aviez jamais visité le site web.
